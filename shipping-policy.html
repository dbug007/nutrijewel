Shipping Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Overview

At NutriJewel, we are committed to delivering our premium, handcrafted nutritious products to you in perfect condition. This shipping policy outlines our delivery process, timelines, and terms.

Important: We currently deliver to select locations within India. All our products are freshly made to order to ensure maximum freshness and quality.

Shipping Coverage

We Currently Deliver To:

  • Pune and surrounding areas
  • Mumbai Metropolitan Region
  • Major cities across Maharashtra
  • Pan-India delivery (selected products)

We are continuously expanding our delivery network. For delivery availability to your location, please contact us before placing your order.

Delivery Timeline

Location Estimated Delivery Time
Pune City 1-2 business days
Pune Outskirts 2-3 business days
Mumbai 2-4 business days
Other Maharashtra Cities 3-5 business days
Other Major Cities 4-7 business days

Note: Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to:

  • Order volume and processing time
  • Weather conditions
  • Public holidays
  • Unforeseen circumstances
  • Remote or difficult to access locations

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on:

  • Delivery location
  • Order weight
  • Order value

Free Shipping:

  • Orders above ₹1,500 within Pune
  • Orders above ₹2,000 for other locations (where applicable)

Exact shipping charges will be displayed at checkout before you complete your order.

Order Processing

  1. Order Confirmation: You will receive an order confirmation via email/SMS within 24 hours
  2. Production: Your order will be freshly prepared (typically 1-2 days)
  3. Quality Check: All products undergo strict quality inspection before packaging
  4. Packaging: Products are packed in food-grade, secure packaging to ensure freshness
  5. Dispatch: You will receive a dispatch notification with tracking details
  6. Delivery: Our logistics partner will deliver to your doorstep

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive:

  • Tracking number via SMS and email
  • Real-time tracking link
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Delivery partner contact information

Delivery Process

Standard Delivery:

  • Delivery between 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM on business days
  • Our delivery partner will attempt delivery twice
  • You will receive a call/SMS before delivery
  • Signature may be required upon delivery

Special Instructions: You can provide special delivery instructions (gate code, preferred time, etc.) while placing your order.

Delivery Attempts: If delivery fails after two attempts, the order will be returned to us. Please ensure someone is available to receive the order, or provide an alternate delivery address.

Packaging

We take extra care in packaging to ensure product safety:

  • Food-Grade Packaging: All products are packed in certified food-grade materials
  • Eco-Friendly: We use sustainable and recyclable packaging wherever possible
  • Temperature Control: Heat-sensitive products are packed with cooling materials
  • Tamper-Proof: All packages are sealed to prevent tampering
  • Labeling: Each package includes product details, batch number, and expiry date

Receiving Your Order

At the time of delivery, please:

  1. Check the package for any visible damage
  2. Verify the order details against your invoice
  3. Inspect the products for any issues
  4. Report any concerns immediately to the delivery person
  5. Keep the invoice and packaging for any future reference
Important: If you notice any damage or issues with the product at the time of delivery, please refuse acceptance and contact us immediately. Do not accept a damaged package.

Undelivered Orders

If your order cannot be delivered due to:

  • Incorrect address provided
  • Recipient unavailable
  • Refused delivery without valid reason

The order will be returned to us, and:

  • A re-delivery can be arranged (additional shipping charges may apply)
  • You can request a refund minus the shipping charges
  • Perishable items cannot be re-delivered (refund minus shipping and 20% restocking fee)

Holidays & Special Occasions

Festival/Holiday Orders:

  • We accept pre-orders for festivals and special occasions
  • Please place orders at least 5-7 days in advance
  • Delivery slots may be limited during peak seasons
  • Processing times may be longer during festival periods

International Shipping

Currently, we do not ship internationally. We only deliver within India. For international orders, please contact us for special arrangements.

Force Majeure

NutriJewel shall not be liable for any delay or failure to deliver due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Strikes or labor disputes
  • Government restrictions
  • Pandemic or health emergencies
  • Transportation disruptions
