Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Overview

At NutriJewel, we take great care in ensuring that our products reach you in perfect condition. All our products are made fresh to order with premium ingredients.

Important: NO REFUNDS POLICY
Due to the perishable and made-to-order nature of our food products, we do NOT accept returns or provide refunds once the order is placed and dispatched.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds or replacements will ONLY be provided in the following exceptional cases where the fault is clearly on our end:

  • Damaged Products: If the product is severely damaged or broken during transit (with photo evidence)
  • Wrong Product: If you receive a completely different product from what you ordered
  • Quality Issues: If the product is spoiled, expired, or severely compromised in quality at the time of delivery
  • Missing Items: If items are completely missing from your order

All claims must be reported within 24 hours of delivery with clear photographic evidence.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following situations:

  • Change of mind or personal preference after order is placed
  • Late reporting (more than 24 hours after delivery)
  • Improper storage of products after delivery
  • Products that have been consumed or partially used
  • Minor cosmetic imperfections that don't affect product quality
  • Taste or flavor preferences
  • Order cancellation after dispatch
  • Issues arising from incorrect address provided by customer
  • Refusal to accept delivery without valid reason
  • Allergic reactions (please check ingredients before ordering)
  • Any dissatisfaction not related to product quality or delivery error
Please Note: By placing an order, you acknowledge and accept our NO REFUNDS policy. We strongly recommend reviewing product details, ingredients, and descriptions carefully before ordering.

How to Report an Issue

If you receive a damaged, wrong, or defective product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Reach out to us within 24 hours of receiving the order
  2. Provide Order Details: Share your order number, date of purchase, and delivery date
  3. Photo Evidence: Send clear photographs of the product showing the issue
  4. Describe the Issue: Explain the problem in detail
Photo Requirements: Please provide clear photos showing:
  • The entire product packaging
  • Close-up of the damage or issue
  • Product label showing batch number and expiry date (if applicable)

Refund Process

  1. Review: Our team will review your refund request within 24-48 hours
  2. Verification: We may contact you for additional information or photos
  3. Approval: Once approved, you will be notified via email/phone
  4. Refund/Replacement: You can choose between:
    • Full refund to your original payment method
    • Product replacement at no additional cost
    • Store credit for future purchases
  5. Processing Time: Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after approval

Refund Amount

Full Refund: You will receive a full refund including:

  • Product cost
  • Delivery charges (if the issue is from our side)
  • Any applicable taxes

Partial Refund: In some cases, we may offer a partial refund based on the extent of the issue.

Replacement Policy

If you opt for a replacement:

  • We will ship the replacement product at no additional cost
  • Replacement will be dispatched within 2-3 business days of approval
  • You will receive tracking information once shipped
  • The defective product may need to be returned (we will arrange pickup)

Cancellation Policy

Before Dispatch: You can cancel your order free of charge before it is dispatched. Full refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.

After Dispatch: Once the order is dispatched, cancellation is not possible. However, you can refuse delivery and request a refund as per our refund policy.

Payment Refund Methods

Refunds will be processed through the original payment method:

  • UPI/Wallet: 2-3 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • Net Banking: 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer (provide account details)
